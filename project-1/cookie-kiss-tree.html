Chocolate Kiss Cookie Trees

Ingredients

  1. Decorations: Hershey Kisses, gummy rope, confectioner's sugar, etc.
  2. Green icing
  3. 1 cup margarine or butter
  4. 1 cup brown sugar
  5. 1 teaspoon vanilla
  6. 3 eggs
  7. 1 teaspoon salt
  8. 1/2 cup powdered milk
  9. 1 cup wheat germ
  10. 1 teaspoon baking soda
  11. 1-1/2 cup flour
  12. 3 cups oatmeal
  13. 3/4 cup chocolate chips

Cooking Instructions

  1. Preset your oven to 350°F. Mix Butter, Brown Sugar, Sugar, Mint (if used), and Vanilla together in a mixing bowl. Add the other ingredients and mix well.
  2. Roll dough into different sized balls and place on baking sheet. Bake for 10 minutes.
  3. Remove from oven and decorate when cool
